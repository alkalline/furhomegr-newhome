Feathered Cowhide Rug

It is a good thing we make all our rugs nonslip; people will be skidding to a stop when they see and feel the quality of this rug. Not only do we use the deepest ebony colored patches of hide, we compliment it by having stark white in the center. To offset the contrast, the black is feathered into the center to avoid giving you harsh lines.

Our artisans spent hours on this design and spend even more time selecting the patches for these types of rugs. Due to the nature of a hide, not a single rug or patch will be exactly the same. There is no better way to decorate your home than with a lavish, elegant rug from Fur Home Rugs.
